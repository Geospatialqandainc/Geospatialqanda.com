ABOUT

Delaware Charitable Nonstock Exempt Corporation

Charitable contributions to IRC 501(c)(3) organizations, except for testing for public safety organizations, are deductible by donors on their federal income tax returns as provided by IRC 170(a)(1).

IRC 509(a)(2) places a limit on the receipt of certain types of income to be regarded as a publicly supported entity. Geospatial Q & A, Inc. normally receives more than one-third of its total support in each taxable year from the sum of: a. Gifts, grants, contributions, volunteer fees; and and b. Gross receipts from admission fees, sales of merchandise, performance of services, or furnishing of facilities, in an activity that is not an unrelated trade or business within the meaning of IRC 513. Disclaimer: Geospatial Q & A, Inc. normally receives less than one-third of its total support from the sum of: a. Gross investment income, and b. Unrelated business taxable income less the tax imposed on that income.

Activities & Exempt Purposes

Description of Our Activities & Exempt Purposes

Scientific

U05 - Research Institutes & Public Policy Analysis
Conduct research and/or public policy analysis.


V25 - Population Studies
Conduct research in the area of social science that involves the systematic study of population models and population phenomena, and related problems of social structure and behavior.

Educational

B90 - Educational
Provide educational programs within the formal educational system or offered as an adjunct to the traditional school curriculum which help students succeed in school and prepare for life. Broker resources for the benefit of local schools.


B80 - Student Services
Support student needs, foster student learning and personal development, create a healthy and diverse community, and enrich student life.

Charitable

I21 - Youth Violence Prevention
Offer a variety of activities for youth who have demonstrated or are at risk for behavior which is likely to be unlawful or to involve them in the juvenile justice system.


S20 - Community & Neighborhood Development
Focus broadly on strengthening, unifying and building the economic, cultural, educational and social services of an urban community or neighborhood.
